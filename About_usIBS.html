Our Vision and Mission

Vision

Learning Excellence with a Community Heart. Our students embrace challenges whilst taking responsibility to achieve their best. They strive to learn the skills and knowledge needed to excel as lifelong learners.

Mission

IBS aims:

  • To create a culture of lifelong learning
  • To deliver a broad, balanced, and challenging education that enables all learners to succeed.
  • To create a safe, stimulating, and engaging learning environment
